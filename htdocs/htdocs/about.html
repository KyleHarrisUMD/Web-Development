Our Principals


- Authenticity

Be yourself. show yourself not only as someone who plays hard but also as someone who works hard, wants a future, and cares deeply about something – music, the environment, animal welfare, tennis, helping people, chess or knitting – whatever it is. self-esteem arises from reminding yourself of what you do well and the good things you work for.

- Diversity

We are all different and many ways exist for being talented. scientist have identified 9 different forms of intelligence. (see _____). we all have a seat at the smart table. value what you do well and pursue it even if you do not want to be a lawyer or hedge fund manager or any supposedly high income job you likely will come to hate.

- Privacy

Our goal is to empower people and make dreams possible and attainable. we need to make money to stay in business but we promise not to treat you as an income stream based on what you say about yourself. we will link you with resources connected to your passions and goals and we want those links to be helpful. it will be your decision if you want to be connected in that way.

- Tricky Times

Recent events have conspired to make getting ahead very difficult: the economic meltdown of the past 7 years, crushing student loan debt laws that can cripple for life, employers who expect unpaid internships as a way to get a foothold in the job market, to name a few. we think these facts of life make life difficult and achieving goals seem like a steep climb. we believe highlighting your talents and accomplishments are more likely to move you forward than liking pictures of kittens posted online or connecting with people who think beyonce’s latest cd is awesome.

- Whats in it for you

Connecting with people who share your passions and interests. advancing a future for you that you want by branding yourself and putting yourself in the best bright light for whatever you want for yourself and to achieve your goals. linking you to people who will help you develop the skills you need and give advice and a site to highlight your talents, passions and skills to companies, institutions and others who are looking for motivated, committed people to hire, admit, or otherwise make a commitment to. and, possibly attract someone willing and able to help pay down your student loan(s).
